Instructions Page


Pre-Conditions

You must run this program in the Safari browser because certain HTML5 tags used are not supported in other browsers. Sidenote: Safari may default to displaying this page in "reader view". Exit this veiw to veiw the entire contents of this page. When you pull this page up in Safari, enable auto-play and pop-up windows for this website.

Back Story

You wake up and find yourself stuck in a strange room with 18 puzzles. Alone with the puzzles and creepy music you descide to take a look.

Instructions

When the first page following this loads, go to the first page on the left to begin the music. Once you are on the page, the music should automatically begin playing. Once the music is running properly note the time left. You have 3 minutes on the clock. Navigate back to the page furthest to your right. Follow instructions on the pages for the individual puzzles. Good Luck.
